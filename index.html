Honey-Lime Grilled Chicken - Put chicken in a ziplock bag. In a small bowl combine lime juice, honey, soy sauce, olive oil, garlic, cilantro, salt and pepper. Preheat your grill to medium high heat. Grill each side of the chicken for about 3-4 minutes or until cooked throughout and no longer pink.

Ratatouille - Heat the oil in a pan over a medium heat. Add the onions and garlic, and cook for 3 minutes. Stir in the courgettes, peppers and aubergine, and cook for a further 5 minutes until lightly coloured. Add the tomatoes and herbs, cover and cook over a low heat for 30-35 minutes until the vegetables are tender.
